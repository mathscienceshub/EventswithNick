About Us

Premium events, planned with care.

Events with Nick is an event management company located at Lephaphane Village, around Lephaphane Clinic. We support clients with event planning, decor styling, and event coordination.

Our work is shaped by elegance, organisation, communication, and attention to detail. Whether the event is corporate, social, cultural, or special, we help turn the client’s idea into a memorable experience.
